# How to Use the New Notifications Tab

## Step-by-Step Guide for Employees

### 1. Navigate to Your Profile
   - Go to **Employees** page
   - Click on your employee card to view your detailed profile

### 2. Access the Notifications Tab
   - On your employee detail page, you'll see two tabs at the top:
     - **Suggestions** (with lightbulb icon 💡)
     - **Notifications** (with bell icon 🔔)
   - Click on the **Notifications** tab

### 3. View Your Rating Progress
   The Notifications tab shows:

   #### Your Current Stats
   - **Total Points Earned**: How many points you have accumulated
   - **Points Needed**: How many more points until you reach 250 (maximum)
   - **Incentive Earned**: Your current incentive status (0% or 5%)

   #### Visual Progress Bar
   - Shows your completion percentage toward the 250-point goal
   - Color updates based on your achievement level

   #### Rating Levels
   Understand where you stand:
   - ⭐ Beginner (0-24)
   - ⭐⭐ Fair (25-74)
   - ⭐⭐⭐ Good (75-149)
   - ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Very Good (150-199)
   - ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Excellent (250+) → **Unlocks 5% Bonus!**

### 4. Understand Your 5% Incentive
   When you reach 250 points:
   - ✓ 5% increased wellness allowance
   - ✓ Priority access to health programs
   - ✓ Recognition in company wellness rankings
   - ✓ Automatic intensity level increase for enhanced tracking

### 5. Check Recent Achievements
   - Scroll down to see your 10 most recent awarded points
   - See who awarded them and when
   - Read the reason for each award

### 6. Celebrate Milestones
   - When you hit 250 points, a green celebration notification appears:
     - 🎉 "Congratulations! You've reached the maximum rating points..."
     - Auto-dismisses in 5 seconds
     - You've earned the 5% wellness incentive!

---

## How Administrators Award Points

1. Navigate to **Ratings** section in the sidebar
2. Select an employee from the list
3. Enter the points to award (typically 10-50 points)
4. Provide a reason for the award
5. Submit the rating

The system will:
- Validate the points with ML analysis
- Save the rating
- Update the employee's total
- Trigger notifications if 250 points is reached
- Automatically increase wellness intensity by 5%
